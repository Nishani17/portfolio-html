My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

1.Sita Ramam

An orphan soldier,Lieutenant Ram's life changes, after he gets a letter from agirl named Sita. He meets her and love blossoms between them .However Ram gets stuck in a camp and the rest will leave you in awe.

2.Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo

Bantu the hero's life gets changed after he gets to know about vaikunthapuram.With lots of mystery the movie is a thorough entertainer

3.Geetha Govindam

Vijay Govind, a well looking decent guy's life changes after he meets a girl at the bus.Things take a turn due to some events.Watch to know how Vijay Govind and Geetha get along together.
